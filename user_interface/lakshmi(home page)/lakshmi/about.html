About Us

Meet our company unless miss the opportunity

The Smart Waste Management System is an innovative solution aimed at transforming urban waste collection and management through the use of advanced technologies such as IoT sensors, machine learning, and real-time data analytics. By equipping public garbage bins with sensors, the system collects real-time data on waste levels, enabling proactive management and significantly reducing overflow incidents. Utilizing predictive analytics, it forecasts future waste generation patterns, allowing for optimized vehicle allocation and tailored collection schedules based on actual demand.
